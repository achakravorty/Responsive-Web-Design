Ada Lovelace

The world's first computer programmer!

A brief timeline of Ada's life

  • 1815 - Augusta Ada Byron is born in London, England.
  • 1828 - Ada writes her first book, "Flyology", about the dynamics of flight.
  • 1833 - Ada meets Charles Babbage, inventor of the Difference Engine and considered a founding father of the computer.
  • 1841 - Ada translates Babbage's lecture about his "Analytical Engine" in English.
  • 1843 - Ada finishes translating and annotating the "Analytical Engine". Her notes are sorted from A to G. Note G is the most famous and describes a computer and its software.
  • 1852 - Ada Lovelace passes away.
